Our Building Process

  1. 1. Initial Consultation & Design

    We start by understanding your vision, budget, and needs. Our architects then create a custom design or adapt an existing plan.

  2. 2. Permitting & Planning

    We handle all necessary permits and ensure the project complies with local building codes and regulations.

  3. 3. Foundation & Framing

    The groundwork begins with site preparation, foundation laying, and erecting the structural framework of your home.

  4. 4. Exterior & Utilities

    Installation of roofing, siding, windows, and doors. Rough-ins for plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems are also completed.

  5. 5. Interior Finishes

    Drywall, flooring, cabinetry, paint, and all interior fixtures are installed, bringing the home to life.

  6. 6. Final Walkthrough & Handover

    A detailed inspection with you to ensure every detail meets your satisfaction before we hand over the keys to your new home.
